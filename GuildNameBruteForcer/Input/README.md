# Dictionary inputs

Dictionary mode downloads the selected Hunspell word list here on its first run:

- `hu_HU.dic` from the [LibreOffice Hungarian dictionary](https://github.com/LibreOffice/dictionaries/tree/master/hu_HU).
- `en_US.dic` from the [LibreOffice English dictionaries](https://github.com/LibreOffice/dictionaries/tree/master/en).

The Hungarian dictionary is copyright László Németh and Ferenc Godó (2025) and is available
under MPL 2.0 or LGPL 3.0-or-later. See the upstream
[README_hu_HU.txt](https://github.com/LibreOffice/dictionaries/blob/master/hu_HU/README_hu_HU.txt)
for its full attribution and licensing notice.

The English dictionary retains its upstream licenses and notices. See
[README_en_US.txt](https://github.com/LibreOffice/dictionaries/blob/master/en/README_en_US.txt) and
[license.txt](https://github.com/LibreOffice/dictionaries/blob/master/en/license.txt).

Downloaded `.dic` files are generated local inputs and are intentionally not committed.
